About Us:

Hello there,
ProfileMakers is a great platform to make your own web profiles completely free without any charges. Our motto is to represent you on the web. This platform is created by Four Developers whom are mentioned below.


Why to create a web profile:

In this era of Internet, it is mandatory to represent yourself on the Internet with your profile, to improve your social contacts, to improve your business, to improve your overall presence !
A resume with a web profile will always stand out from those ordinary resumes where only the social site links are provided.
You can represent yourself more comprehensively with a web profile !


Your Privacy:

We strongly ensure you that, all the information provided by you will be used just to build your profile and will not be sold or kept for an organisation.
The profile information provided by you is used by our bots to generate your web page, by embedding this information in your web profile.


What you will get:

We don't want our clients to get entangled in those so called flashy iconic interfaces. We are providing you with a simple but attractive interface, where you can collect your own we profile just with 2 to 3 clicks. You just have to fill a simple form and click on proceed to host your web profile on our sever or you can download the profile on your own personal computer also.
